FishCoin Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Fishcoin! We wrote this policy to help you understand what information we collect, how we use it and what choices you have. We welcome your questions and comments on this policy.

What information do we collect?

We collect information in a few different ways:

  • When you give it to us or give us permission  - To use our product you voluntarily give us certain information. This can include your email address, phone number, location, call logs and message logs and your contacts. You may also give us permission to access your information in other services eg. Facebook, Twitter or Google.
  • Technical information when you use our product - These days whenever you use a website, mobile application or other internet services, there's certain information that almost always gets created and recorded automatically. The same is true of our products. This includes device information, applications you use on your device and other log data.

How do we use the information we collect?

We use the information we collect to provide our products to you, make them better, develop new products and protect our users. For example we collect call and message logs to determine how much you use your phone and this helps us manage your mobile account efficiently for you.

What choices do you have about your information?

Our product requires personal information to function. You can choose to unlink your account by uninstalling our product. Please write to us (see below) to remove all your information from our server.

How and when do we share information?

We never share your information with anybody except for the following cases:

  • When you buy something in our product we pass on information to the payment provider and the merchant.
  • If we believe that disclosure is reasonably necessary to comply with a law or regulation to protect the safety, rights, or property of the public, any person, or FishCoin; or to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.
  • We may engage in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution, reorganization, or similar transaction or proceeding that involves the transfer of the information described in this Policy.

We may also share aggregated or non-personally identifiable information with our partners, advertisers, or others.

How to contact us regarding privacy issues?

Please contact us at support@tinkerix.com for any concerns, issues or questions.
